Frequently Ask Questions

How do I place an Order?

You can place an order via email, phone call or visit to our store.

How much does shipping cost?

Shipping charges vary based on your location and the products you have purchased. You can contact us for more accurate estimate of shipping charges.

How will I know if my order has been received?

If you have emailed a purchase order to sweetbites@gmail.com, a confirmation email will be sent to you once the order has been processed during our regular office hours Monday to Friday 9am – 5pm. If you have faxed a purchase order to 03-450-7890, a confirmation email or fax will be sent to you once the order has been processed during our regular office hours Monday to Friday 9am – 5pm.

How do I cancel my order?

If you need to cancel your order please contact us at 03-450-7890. Please keep in mind that we strive to process and ship orders the same day (if received before 1pm), and once the order has shipped you will be subject to our return policy.

When will my products arrive?

Our fastest shipping to Malaysia takes just 1-2 days when you choose an express delivery service. And if you're shipping on a budget, our economy shipping options take between 3-12 days depending on the Malaysia courier service you choose.
